How It Works

From connected source to governed pipeline in six steps.

A concrete rollout path, not a philosophy. Here's what happens when you bring a new data source into Bitool.

1

Connect your source

Point Bitool at an API (REST, GraphQL) or provide an OpenAPI spec. The system discovers endpoints, detects authentication patterns, and identifies pagination strategies automatically.

Connector intelligence: Auth type detection (Bearer, OAuth, API key), cursor/offset/keyset pagination inference, rate limit awareness, retry configuration. You configure exceptions, not defaults.
2

Infer Bronze schema

Bitool samples live data, infers column types, suggests primary keys and watermark fields, and configures incremental loading. You review and adjust — AI explains every recommendation.

What you get: Field types, nullability, nested structure flattening, watermark selection with rationale, primary key candidates with confidence scores, schema evolution mode (append/merge).
3

Generate Silver & Gold proposals

From your Bronze tables, Bitool proposes cleaned Silver dimension and fact tables, then Gold business models — KPIs, aggregations, marts. AI-assisted, but every proposal is a reviewable diff.

Bronze — raw API data Silver — cleaned, typed, deduplicated Gold — KPIs, aggregations, marts
4

Review, validate, and publish

Every change follows the governance lifecycle. Proposals are compiled to SQL deterministically, validated against schema and business rules, reviewed by your team, and published as immutable versioned artifacts.

Execute on your warehouse

Published pipelines compile to SQL and execute on Databricks, Snowflake, or PostgreSQL. The compiler is deterministic — same graph always produces the same SQL. No AI in the execution path.

Supported targets: Databricks (SQL + Jobs API), Snowflake, PostgreSQL. Warehouse-native execution — no proprietary runtime.

Monitor in production

The Ops Console gives you real-time visibility across the entire medallion chain. Not just "did it run" — freshness, drift, quality, and lineage.

Semantic Layer

Your business logic, browsable and governed.

Bitool auto-generates a Semantic Model from your existing Silver/Gold pipeline — entity classification, column roles, measures, hierarchies, and join relationships. Browse it, edit it in English, govern it with approval workflows.

Auto-generated from your pipeline

One click assembles a Semantic Model from your Silver/Gold proposals, schema context, and FK relationships. Entities are classified as fact or dimension. Column roles (business_key, measure, attribute, time_dimension) are inferred. Confidence scores tell you how certain each inference is.

Calculated & restricted measures

Define cost_per_mile = fuel_cost / NULLIF(miles, 0) once, query by name forever. Restricted measures like emea_revenue add automatic WHERE clauses. Both become first-class options in AI Reporting — the LLM picks from your defined measures, not raw columns.

Perspectives & hierarchies

Create audience-scoped views: finance sees revenue measures, operations sees operational metrics. Define drill hierarchies (region → city → store) and the AI suggests drill-down queries. Time hierarchies auto-expand to year/quarter/month/week/day.

Governance built in

Models follow a full lifecycle: draft → submit for review (with designated reviewer) → approve/reject → publish. Row-level security policies restrict data per user session. Impact analysis shows affected reports before you change a model. Workspace-scoped promotion moves models from dev to production.

Edit in English: "Add measure cost_per_mile as fuel_cost / miles on trips." The system parses, validates, and applies. No YAML, no SQL config files, no LookML.

How it works

AI Assistance

AI that helps you build, not AI that builds for you.

Bitool's AI suggests schemas, explains proposals, and drafts transformations. But every suggestion is constrained by the compiler and validated by your governance process.

What AI does

  • ✓ Suggests primary keys and watermarks with confidence scores
  • ✓ Explains any node or proposal in plain English
  • ✓ Drafts Silver and Gold model proposals from Bronze schemas
  • ✓ Explains validation failures with suggested fixes
  • ✓ Explains schema drift impact and suggests remediation
  • ✓ Answers natural-language questions about your data (ISL pipeline)
  • ✓ Generates transform graphs from business descriptions
  • ✓ Edits semantic models in plain English

What AI doesn't do

  • ✗ Generate unconstrained SQL that you have to validate
  • ✗ Execute in production — the compiler is fully deterministic
  • ✗ Bypass governance gates or skip validation
  • ✗ Mutate pipelines without proposal review
  • ✗ Access data outside the governed schema
  • ✗ Replace your team's judgment — it always proposes, never decides

No hallucinated SQL

The AI generates a constrained ISL (Intelligent Structured Language) document, not raw SQL. Table names are enum-locked to your actual schema. Every column and join is validated before compilation. Invalid queries are rejected with a clear explanation — they never reach your database.

Semantic model awareness

When a published semantic model exists, reporting automatically uses it. Calculated measures like cost_per_mile, restricted measures, dimension hierarchies, and lazy associations become the vocabulary the AI picks from. Row-level security policies are enforced transparently per user session.

Seven supported backends

PostgreSQL, MySQL, Oracle, SQL Server, DB2, Snowflake, and Databricks. Auto-training detects common SaaS schemas (Stripe, HubSpot, Salesforce, Shopify, QuickBooks, GA4, Jira, Zendesk) instantly — no LLM calls needed for recognized sources.

Saved reports & follow-ups

Save any report with its ISL, semantic model binding, and perspective. Re-executing restores the full context including RLS enforcement. Ask follow-up questions — Bitool maintains conversational context across your session. Auto-charting detects patterns and suggests visualizations.
