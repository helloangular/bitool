Segment 03
Mid-Market
201–2,000 employees · BI team of 3–10 · Series C+
No Migration Required
Headline Message
"One source of truth. Zero migration. Keep your warehouse."
Your BI team already spent two years building the warehouse. You have Snowflake, you have dbt models, you have a governance committee. What you don't have is a way for the other 1,800 employees to self-serve without breaking it. Bitool sits on top of your existing warehouse, introspects your live schema on every query, and gives every employee AI-guided querying that can never write invalid SQL. Your BI team becomes the curator, not the bottleneck.
Pain We Attack
  • $400k Tableau bill, still only 80 active users
  • Analysts are ticket-takers instead of strategists
  • "Text-to-SQL" tools hallucinate and leak columns
  • Every department wants Copilot but legal won't allow it
  • Two-year dbt project, still can't answer "revenue by region, last quarter"
What They Get
  • Connects to Snowflake/Databricks/Postgres in 10 minutes
  • Dynamic schema introspection on every query — always current
  • Foreign-key-aware joins on Postgres; guided joins everywhere else
  • Per-team scopes: Finance sees finance, Ops sees ops
  • Every query logged, auditable, re-executable
  • Saved reports become the new "certified dashboards"
Buyer & Trigger
  • Economic buyer: Head of Data / VP Analytics
  • Champion: Senior Analyst drowning in Slack requests
  • Trigger: failed or disappointing text-to-SQL pilot
  • Trigger: board asked "where's our AI story for analytics?"
  • Decision cycle: 6–12 weeks with security review
Channels That Work
Outbound to VP Data/Analytics Snowflake Summit / Databricks DAIS Gartner-style comparison content Customer advocacy + logos Analyst relations (Forrester/Gartner) POC-to-paid motion
$2,499 /month · up to 250 seats · SSO · audit log
Segment 04
Enterprise
2,000+ employees · Chief Data Officer · Multi-warehouse
Zero-Hallucination Guarantee
Headline Message
"The only AI analytics tool your CISO will approve."
At your scale, "AI that sometimes hallucinates a column" is a fireable offense. Bitool's ISL pipeline is architecturally incapable of hallucinating — every query is validated against your live schema, compiled deterministically, and scoped to the tables a user is permitted to see. The LLM never writes SQL directly. Every answer is audit-logged. Every saved report is re-executable for regulatory review. Self-hosted, VPC-isolated, BYO-LLM.
Pain We Attack
  • CISO blocks every generative AI analytics trial
  • Compliance: SOX, HIPAA, GDPR — need audit trail on every query
  • Different business units on different warehouses
  • Shadow IT: execs bribing analysts for numbers
  • Existing "enterprise BI" = $2M/year, 6-month rollouts, still not adopted
What They Get
  • Self-hosted or dedicated VPC deployment
  • BYO-LLM: Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, private Claude deployment
  • ISL compilation guarantees: no raw LLM SQL ever reaches the DB
  • Row-level security + column masking passthrough
  • SAML SSO, SCIM provisioning, full audit log stream
  • White-glove onboarding + dedicated CSM
Buyer & Trigger
  • Economic buyer: CDO / CIO
  • Technical buyer: Head of Data Platform
  • Gatekeeper: CISO + Legal + Procurement
  • Trigger: "AI analytics" on the board's 2026 priorities
  • Trigger: failed Copilot-for-Data pilot this year
  • Decision cycle: 3–9 months, security questionnaire + SOC 2 required
Channels That Work
Named-account outbound Executive roundtables SI partnerships (Deloitte, Accenture) Cloud marketplace (AWS/Azure/GCP) Industry analyst briefings CDO/CIO peer networks (Evanta, Gartner) Security-first positioning papers
Custom starting $150k/year · unlimited seats · dedicated support
